About Campus Connect

Connecting university students for easier scheduling and coordination on campus.

Our Mission

Campus Connect is designed to make scheduling and social coordination on campus more seamless. We understand that managing a busy academic and social life can be challenging, and our goal is to help students maximize their time by finding the best ways to meet up, study, and attend events together. Through real-time updates and integration with university schedules, Campus Connect brings students closer to their goals and their peers.

Our Vision

We envision a campus where students can effortlessly coordinate with each other, helping them make the most of their college experience. Campus Connect aims to create a sense of community by facilitating easy planning for study sessions, group projects, and social events. Our goal is to break down barriers to connection and make every moment on campus more meaningful.

Meet Our Team

For prototype testing, he pre-loads sample student schedules with consent. His efforts make sure that Campus Connect provides reliable and synchronized data for users, enabling them to plan around their existing commitments effortlessly.
